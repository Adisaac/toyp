About the Ten Outstanding Young Persons (TOYP) Program

The Ten Outstanding Young Persons (TOYP) program is an internationally recognized initiative that highlights young individuals excelling in various fields, including leadership, innovation, entrepreneurship, and humanitarian work.

Our Purpose

The TOYP award ceremony seeks to recognize and celebrate young changemakers, inspire the next generation of leaders, and provide a platform for networking and mentorship.

History of TOYP

The TOYP award has been honoring exceptional young individuals for years, providing them with a platform to expand their impact and access new opportunities.

2000

TOYP was founded as a way to celebrate young individuals creating positive change.

2015

Expanded to include multiple categories such as Leadership, Business, and Social Impact.

2025

New mentorship programs introduced to support awardees beyond the ceremony.
